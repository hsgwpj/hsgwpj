ABOUT　US

Wine In University is a league of university students who are interested in wine. It was founded by its present chair person Zhang Teng in May 2014. In the second half year of 2014, 4 different theme tastings were hold which contained 10 universities' students in Shaanxi Province with our effort. With evolution, most universities who set up enology college in China have joined us in the first half year of 2015. And this marks that WINE IN UNIVERSITY had been spreading in the whole country. Now, we hold both online and offline activities every month at 9 different provinces.

THE SIGNIFICANCE OF US

  1. Gather student wine lovers, and they can study and taste together on our platform. They can also share more sources of wine and knowledge.
  2. Make the communication among universities around the country more frequently and easily.
  3. Buildbridges between university students and wine company.

THE FORM OF ACTIVITIES

  1. Offline Activities: Theme tastings once a month —— to be hold at the same time in the country.Wines and teachers are provided by the sponsors. There will be several sections in the activities, such as teaching, tastings and prizes games. We will give publicity to the whole activities by press release, photos, and even videos.
  2. Online Activities: Wechat Class twice per month —— to invite some important people in wine business to give a lecture to university students.

ORGANIZATION　STRUCTURE

  1. Chair Person: to engage in coordinating the universities and the sponsors.
  2. Publicity Director: to give publicity to everything about us.
  3. Leader of University: to organize the Offline Activities in their school.
  4. Wine in University Advisor: Li Deimei
